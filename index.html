20th Century Cocktail

Ingredients:

  • ¾ oz / 22 ½ mL Lemon juice
  • ½ oz / 15 mL Crème de Cacao
  • ¾ oz / 22 ½ mL Lillet blanc
  • 1 ½ oz / 45 mL London Dry Gin
  • Lemon twist

Directions:

  1. Add ice, lemon juice, creme de cacao, lillet blanc, and gin to shaker and shake thoroughly
  2. Strain into chilled nick and nora glass
  3. Garnish with twist of lemon
